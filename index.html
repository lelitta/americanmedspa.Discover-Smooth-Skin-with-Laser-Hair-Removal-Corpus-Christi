What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal (also known as laser hair reduction) is the process of utilizing specially designed laser technology to permanently remove unwanted hair. This allows individuals the freedom to no longer worry about hair removal methods, such as waxing and shaving, which can cause irritation and leave behind stubbles of hair. With laser hair removal, the results are long-lasting, revealing a smoother complexion year-round.

How Does Laser Hair Removal Work?

Imagine a world where you never have to worry about shaving or waxing again. Laser hair removal makes this a reality! During the treatment, a carefully targeted beam of light is directed at the area where you want unwanted hair gone. This powerful light is absorbed by the pigment in your hair, converting into heat that targets and destroys the hair follicle—without harming the surrounding skin.

Once a follicle is destroyed, it can’t grow back, making this treatment a long-term solution to your hair removal needs. With advanced laser systems designed for precision, each treatment is tailored to your unique skin tone, hair color, and hair thickness, ensuring the best results possible.     Preparing for Your Laser Hair Removal Appointment

    For the best results, follow these preparation tips:

    • Shave the area to be treated the day before or the day of treatment.
    • Avoid tanning and self-tanners for 4 weeks before and 2 weeks after your treatments.
    • Avoid bleaching, waxing, or tweezing the area for at least 4 weeks prior.
    • Refrain from sun exposure for 6 weeks before and after treatment
    • Avoid blood-thinning medications, such as aspirin and anti-inflammatory drugs, prior to treatment.
